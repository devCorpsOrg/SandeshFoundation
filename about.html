About Us

Sandesh Foundation is a not for profit organization registered under Indian Trust Act 1882 in Delhi (NCT), registered in 2014. Since last 6 years, Sandesh Foundation is working on Education, Health & Nutrition, Women Empowerment, Relief activities like food and cloths distribution to needed especially at the time of disaster and pandemic like Covid-19. And we are also working with different Govt. activities and schemes like immunization, polio, vaccination and Health Camps and Health Checkups. Working with Govt. Schools for supporting needed community and supporting education. 

Our Vision

We aim to work towards creating a better India which provides basic health, education and empowerment to every citizen and be always there for one's in need. 
Our mission is to empower economically backward children through knowledge, health care, education, vocational training and commitment and the right set of values to take it forward.
